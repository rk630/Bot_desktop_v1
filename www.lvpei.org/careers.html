Careers @ LVPEI

At L V Prasad Eye Institute (LVPEI), we are committed to promoting good vision, preventing blindness, and developing innovative techniques that can potentially revolutionize eye care services. The right person for any job in LVPEI will contribute to these fundamental goals. We offer a wide range of job opportunities in medical, paramedical, and related support service areas. You will be sure to benefit from a safe, productive, purposeful, and responsive environment that fosters excellent performance at the individual and team levels. Receiving equal treatment, having access to a variety of opportunities, gaining from periodic re-orientation to learning and development. Here, you can expect to proudly imbibe the organisational culture, and likely thrive on it both personally and professionally.

Suitably-qualified individuals are invited to apply for the positions listed below:
  • Eye Donation Counselor
  • Supervisor - Human Resources
  • Assistant - Operating Room

Eye Donation Counselor

Nature of Job

Responsible for approaching donor families and obtaining consent for donation prior to recovery of tissue and coordinating timely and effective corneal recovery.

Required skills

  • Excellent oral and written communication skills with the ability to interact effectively with all Eye Bank and hospital staff.
  • Must be able to effectively communicate and collaborate with peers while fostering a positive work environment.
  • Able to interpret and follow written and oral direction.
  • Able to effectively persuade individuals and groups.
  • Must be able to work long hours, nights and Sundays/holidays when required
  • Personal commitment to eye and organ donation.
Qualification
  • Intermediate/ Degree preferably from science back round (like MLT, BiPC, GNM, BSC )
Location
Hyderabad (must be open to travel to other locations too)
Experience
  • not mandatory

Interested Candidates should send their applications to hr@lvpei.org

Supervisor - Human Resources

Nature of Job

LV Prasad Eye Institute is seeking to employ individuals in Human Resource Department.

Job Description

  • Managing Payroll related legal & statutory Compliance (PF, ESIC, PT, LWF & Minimum wages)
  • Ensure timely preparation and submission of various statutory forms and returns related to ESI, EPF, Gratuity, labour welfare fund etc.
  • Ensure legal payments such as Provident Fund, ESI, Professional Tax and all other government/statutory compliance as applicable are paid in time and the reports pertaining to them are submitted on time.
Experience
  • Should have 2-3 years of relevant experience in handling all the statutory matters (PF, ESI. P. Tax, LWF, Gratuity, Minimum Wages etc.) in particular. Preferably a male candidate with a two wheeler and a valid driving license.
Others
  • Should be enthusiastic and good in communications both written and verbal (English, Hindi & Telugu)
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel

Interested Candidates should send their applications to hr@lvpei.org

Assistant - Operating Room

Nature of Job

LV Prasad Eye Institute is seeking to employ Assistants in Operating Room.

Job Description

  • To sterilize the packs for next morning and to keep everything ready for next day's work.
  • Should know the sterilization recall policy
  • To check the linen every evening- like supervising the number of each item of linen brought by the washer man and give the soiled linen to the washer man and maintain the register.
  • To make sure that unsterile things are not available in the sterile zone
Qualification
  • Intermediate preferably from science back round (like MLT, BiPC)
Experience
  • Not mandatory

Interested Candidates should send their applications to hr@lvpei.org
