Fellowship

Eligibility Criteria: MS, MD, DNB, DO, DOMS

Duration for national candidates: 36 months (January 01, 2025 to December 31, 2027)

Duration for international candidates: 12 months

 

Standard Chartered – LVPEI, Academy for Eye Care Education, invites applications for Long Term Fellowship in the subspecialties of Cornea and Anterior Segment, Retina & Vitreous, Glaucoma, Strabismus, Pediatric & Neuroophthalmology, Oculoplasty & Ocular oncology, Uveitis, Cataract & Refractive Surgeries.

This unique 3 H's fellowship is aimed to train budding ophthalmologists to become a self sufficient and excellent super specialty practitioner.
*3 H's(HANDS: Surgical competency HEAD: Critical thinking HEART: Serving the underprivileged)

The fellowship is an excellent blend of clinical rotation, surgical training and research.

At the end of the training program, the trainee will be able to:

  • Provide excellent comprehensive management of ocular diseases.
  • Manage all the predominant ailments in the subspecialty independently
  • Become an expert in various surgical techniques
  • Be able to publish research papers


Rotations:
* First 9 months - Advanced induction module, Basic sub specialty mentor based training along with intensive exit competency based training programs & become an expert in various cataract surgery techniques
* 12 months - Opportunity to manage a secondary centre
* 15 months - Advanced Sub-specialty training
