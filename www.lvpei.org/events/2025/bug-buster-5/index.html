We are excited to invite you to the fifth edition of Bug Busters, a highly anticipated event dedicated to exploring the latest advancements in corneal infection research. This prestigious meeting brings together leading experts, researchers, and clinicians from around the world to exchange knowledge, present groundbreaking findings, and discuss cutting-edge strategies in the field of corneal infections.

Corneal infections pose significant challenges to eye health, and finding effective solutions requires a multidisciplinary approach. Bug Busters 5 aims to foster collaboration among ophthalmologists, microbiologists, immunologists, and other professionals working in this domain. Through engaging discussions, interactive sessions, and thought-provoking presentations, we will delve into the complex nature of corneal infections and explore innovative approaches to diagnosis, treatment, and prevention.

Meeting Highlights:

  • Workshops on microbiology skills, staining, and diagnostics
  • Interactive case panels & expert debates
  • AI & molecular tools in diagnosing corneal infections
  • Live surgical demos and expert video discussions
  • Competitions: Poster, Paper, Video & Photography

We look forward to your participation in Bug Busters 5: Corneal Infection Meeting. Join us in this enlightening and invigorating event, where we aim to uncover new insights, advance our collective understanding, and make significant strides towards combating corneal infections.

Save the date and stay tuned for further updates on the agenda, speakers, registration, and more. Together, let's conquer the challenges of corneal infections and pave the way for a brighter future for eye health!
