Long-Term Fellowship 2 Faculty program

Eligibility: MS, DNB, MD, or equivalent postgraduate qualification in Ophthalmology

Duration: 36 months of immersive training and mentorship (October 01, 2025 to September 30, 2028)

 

Calling all aspiring ophthalmologists who dream to shape the future of eye care!

The Standard Chartered – LVPEI Academy of Eye Care Education invites you to apply to our unique and exciting  long-term fellowship 2 faculty program . This is your chance to hone your professional competence and reach four important milestones in your professional career.

Program Highlights

First milestone
Clinical Ophthalmologist: Gain in-depth experience across the sub-specialty of your choice.

Second milestone
Academic Ophthalmologist: A developmental program during the fellowship is designed to cultivate future academic Ophthalmologists with emphasis on innovation, technology, education, research, community eye care etc, providing a direct pathway to a rewarding academic career.

Third milestone
Leadership in Ophthalmology: Neatly intertwined within the fabric of the program is knowing, understanding, and applying the legacy of LVPEI through its founding philosophy, vision, mission, five values (excellence, equity, patient first, togetherness and integrity) and culture.

Fourth milestone
Recruited as a faculty member at one of the tertiary centres of LVPEI.
