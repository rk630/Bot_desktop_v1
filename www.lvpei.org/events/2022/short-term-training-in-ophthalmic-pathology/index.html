    Short Term Training in Ophthalmic Pathology

    Apply Now

    Ophthalmic Pathology is a highly specialized branch of pathology dealing exclusively with ophthalmic tissues. The primary objective of this training program is to train and teach ophthalmologists and pathologists, the basics of Ophthalmic Pathology and enabling them to deal and diagnose pathology specimens in their daily practise.

    Who can apply?

    Certified ophthalmologists or pathologists with MD/MS/DNB degree

    Program Highlights

       Basic orientation in Ophthalmic Pathology
       Didactic lectures
       Sub-specialty case presentations
       Grossing techniques
       Ancillary techniques

    Program details

       Duration: 3 months
       Batches to start every January, April, July & October
       Course fee (including GST): 118,000 INR
                (exclusive of accommodation and food)

    After completion of the 3-month training program, the candidate will be eligible for certification in
    Short Term Training in Specialty.
