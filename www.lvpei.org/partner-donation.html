Donate Now

Partner with us

so that all may see

We are committed “To provide equitable and efficient eye care to all sections of society.” You can help LVPEI in its endeavor to prevent blindness and provide vision rehabilitation by getting involved in any of the following ways.

The Challenges

India is home to nearly 25% of the world’s blind population.

Our Vision

To provide equitable and efficient eye care to all sections of society.

The Solution

Individuals, corporates and other organizations partner with us to expand care to those who need eyecare. Your donation enables LVPEI to provide more than 50% of services free of cost to the economically underprivileged.

Make A Donation

Your support helps us provide 50% of our services for those who cannot afford to pay.

Your financial contribution towards LVPEI’s mission could be utilized in several ways: Eye care services for non-paying patients in rural areas, training deserving eye care professionals, researching eye disease treatment strategies, or setting up new facilities at the Institute.
